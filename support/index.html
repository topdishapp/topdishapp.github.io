Support

Top Dish helps you find the best dish at restaurants near you. We're a small team and read every message.

Contact us

Questions, bug reports, or feedback? Email topdishapplication@gmail.com and we'll get back to you. You can also send feedback right from the app: Profile → Send Feedback.

Common questions

How do I delete my account? Open your profile (the avatar in the top-right corner) and tap Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and data.

Do I need an account? No — you can browse without signing in. Signing in (with Apple, Google, or email) lets you save spots and sync across devices.

Something on a listing looks wrong? Open the restaurant, tap the ⋯ button, and choose Report a Problem — we review every report.

Why is some content "coming soon"? We're adding dish analysis for more restaurants continuously. More spots and cities are on the way.

Privacy

See our Privacy Policy. We show no ads, use no trackers, and your location never leaves your device.
